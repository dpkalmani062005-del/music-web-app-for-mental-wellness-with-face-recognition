Place face-api.js models in this folder.

Required files (TinyFaceDetector and FaceExpressionNet):
- tiny_face_detector_model-weights_manifest.json
- tiny_face_detector_model-shard1.bin
- face_expression_model-weights_manifest.json
- face_expression_model-shard1.bin

You can obtain compatible models from: https://github.com/justadudewhohacks/face-api.js/tree/master/weights
Copy them into this directory and keep the filenames exactly as above.
